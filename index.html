About

Darcy's Piano Tuition, located in Camberwell, Melbourne, offers professional piano lessons for all skill levels. Darcy provides personalised instruction for beginners and advanced learners alike, fostering both technical skill and musical appreciation in a supportive environment.

Darcy's journey as a piano tutor reflects a lifelong passion for music and dedication to education. After studying at The University of Melbourne, he developed his skills through rigorous training and performances. His teaching philosophy emphasizes personalised guidance that nurtures both technical proficiency and a deep appreciation for music. Darcy also engages in community events and workshops, inspiring students to explore their musical potential.

Lessons

Lessons cater to all levels, from beginners to advanced students. Darcy ensures each student receives tailored guidance to progress confidently at their own pace, inspiring growth and a lifelong love for piano playing.

Lessons are held at Darcy's studio in Camberwell on weekdays between 3 pm–8 pm, and Saturdays 9 am–12 pm. Prices: $50 for 30 minutes or $90 for 50 minutes. Regular weekly lessons are recommended for steady progress. Contact Darcy to discuss scheduling or questions about lessons.
